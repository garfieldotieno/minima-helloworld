Frequently Asked Questions

What is The Safe?

The Safe allows you to have a separate seed phrase to lock up the majority of your coins - this could be Minima or other custom tokens. This means you can have a wallet for day-to-day spending (whatever you do not send to a Safe) and additional segregated wallets (Safes) on the same node. You can then leave your node unlocked for daily use whilst keeping the majority of your holdings secured with an alternative seed phrase.

How do I unlock a Safe?

To unlock a Safe, you will need to enter the seed phrase you provided when creating the Safe.

Does this mean I have multiple seed phrases?

Yes you will have your main seed phrase and your Safe seed phrase(s) - you must keep them both safe in a secure location.

What should I do if I lose my node?

If you lose your node you can recover your daily wallet and Safe by importing your main seed phrase using the Archive Reset in the Security MiniDapp.

If my main seed phrase is compromised, are the coins in my Safe safe?

Yes, provided you used a different seed phrase for your Safe! In this situation DO NOT UNLOCK YOUR SAFE TO YOUR MAIN NODE, instead start a new node and then import your Safe seed phrase.
